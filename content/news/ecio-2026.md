PAL members are presenting at the **European Conference on Integrated Optics (ECIO) 2026**, held at ETH Zurich Zentrum in Zurich, Switzerland!

We are excited to share our latest work in integrated photonic computing with the ECIO community, with one oral presentation and one poster covering
- **Integrated Photonic Computing**: Photonic hardware for electroencephalography signal forecasting
- **Spectral Photonic Encoding**: A spectral photonic encoder for computational tasks via optoelectronic recurrence

We look forward to seeing you there!

<!--more-->

### Presentations

- **Integrated Photonic Computing for Electroencephalography Signal Forecasting** (paper T2C.2)
- **Spectral Photonic Encoder for Computational Tasks via Optoelectronic Recurrence** (paper P12.15)
