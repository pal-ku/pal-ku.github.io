PAL members are heading to Strasbourg for **SPIE Photonics Europe 2026**!

We are excited to share our latest work in integrated photonics and optical computing with the European photonics community. This year, we have two presentations in the **Machine Learning in Photonics II** track:

- **Learned Manifold Reparameterization**: A learned manifold reparameterization enabling DRC-compatible inverse design in silicon photonics
- **Reconfigurable Deep Photonic Recurrent Networks**: Reconfigurable deep photonic recurrent networks for optoelectronic information processing

We look forward to seeing you in Strasbourg!
